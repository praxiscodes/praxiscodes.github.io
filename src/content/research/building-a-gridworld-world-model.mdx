Gridworld looks trivial until latent dynamics fail to preserve wall collisions and terminal transitions.

## Why start with Gridworld

- Fully observable.
- Deterministic transitions.
- Easy to audit prediction errors step-by-step.

## Practical setup

State is represented as one-hot position plus orientation, with action space `{up, down, left, right}`.
The world model is a two-layer MLP trained on transition tuples `(s_t, a_t, s_{t+1}, r_t, done_t)`.

## Primary metrics

1. One-step transition accuracy
2. Multi-step rollout fidelity
3. Reward prediction precision

## Main failure pattern

Compounding error appears after 8-12 imagined steps, especially near sparse reward boundaries.
This suggests that short-horizon prediction quality does not imply planning quality.
