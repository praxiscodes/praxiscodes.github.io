RSSMs combine two signals:

1. A deterministic recurrent memory for long context.
2. A stochastic latent variable for uncertainty and multimodality.

The deterministic hidden state keeps trajectory continuity, while the stochastic state captures ambiguity.

## Useful decomposition

Let $h_t$ be deterministic and $z_t$ stochastic.

$$
h_t = f(h_{t-1}, z_{t-1}, a_{t-1}),\qquad z_t \sim p(z_t\mid h_t)
$$

Posterior inference adds observation conditioning:

$$
z_t \sim q(z_t\mid h_t, x_t)
$$

This split keeps imagination stable while still encoding uncertainty.
