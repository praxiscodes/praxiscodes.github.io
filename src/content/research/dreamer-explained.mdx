Dreamer replaces expensive environment interaction with latent imagination rollouts.

## Core loop

1. Learn world model from replay.
2. Imagine trajectories inside latent space.
3. Optimize actor and critic on imagined trajectories.
4. Collect fresh data and repeat.

The gain comes from reusing each real interaction many times through model-based rollouts.

## Watchpoint

If value estimates become overconfident in imagined states, policy quality drops quickly in the real environment.
